Website FAQ

What types of pizza do you offer?

- We offer a variety of pizzas including pepperoni, Hawaiian, veggie, margherita, and more.

Do you have gluten-free options?

- Yes, we offer gluten-free crust options for our pizzas.

What are your hours of operation?

- Our hours of operation are Monday-Sunday from 11:00am to 9:00pm.

Can I place an order for delivery?

- Yes, we offer delivery within a 5-mile radius for a small fee.

Do you offer vegan or vegetarian options?

- Yes, we offer vegan and vegetarian options including our veggie pizza and vegan cheese option.

How long does it take to make a pizza?

- It takes approximately 10-15 minutes to make a pizza.
